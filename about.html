About Us


Aguy Auto Sales is an automotive electronic commerce, it describes the online purchasing and selling of automobiles and their parts. Aguy aims to provide citizens with reliable and affordable automobiles for day-to-day recreation, and the team behind the company are Arnold Lim, Gabriel Dy, and Killian Vitug.


Gabriel Dy

UX/UI Designer

Gab is in charge of mapping out the website in a raw sketch (a wireframe) so it’s clear for the visitors how they can use the website and navigate through it. The graphic designer creates a visual style for the website aligned with the corporate style guide. He also creates a visual design for each functionality as a guideline for the front-end developer, who will re-create this design by code.

Arnold Lim

Quality Tester

Working closely with Gab, Arnold tests every functionality that the team has made in every possible condition before the website becomes available to regular users. When something is not in order, he reports a bug to the developers. The tester does not simply click around in the application but follows a strict plan and procedure to make sure they will test every possibility.

Killian Vitug

Project Manager/Analyst

Killian provides focus for the team on various projects, and help to keep it moving along on schedule. He manages the budget, takes care of planning, and ensures the team is staffed with the best talent. As the team's analyst, he is in charge of translating the requests (requirements) made by the client into clear specifications on how a certain functionality should behave.
